Nelson Mandela

A global symbol of peace, justice, and freedom

Timeline of Mandela’s Life

  • 1918 – Born in Mvezo, South Africa.
  • 1944 – Co-founded the African National Congress Youth League.
  • 1962 – Arrested and sentenced to life imprisonment for fighting apartheid.
  • 1990 – Released after 27 years in prison.
  • 1993 – Awarded the Nobel Peace Prize.
  • 1994 – Became South Africa’s first democratically elected president.
  • 2013 – Passed away at age 95, leaving a legacy of freedom and equality.
